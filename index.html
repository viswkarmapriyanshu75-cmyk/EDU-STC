‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What age can children start robotics?

Children as young as 5 can start with our beginner programs. We have age-appropriate courses for all skill levels up to 16 years old.

Do children need prior programming experience?

No! All our courses start from the basics. Our instructors guide complete beginners through every step.

Are materials included in the courses?

Yes! All robotics kits, components, and materials are included in the course fees. No hidden costs.

How often are classes held?

Classes run on weekends (2-4 hours per week) and during summer with intensive camps available.

Can I purchase kits separately?

Absolutely! You can buy our robotics kits independently even without enrolling in courses.
